About the project

Quantum Console is a quantum computing simulator made as an introdutiry implementation for students interested in the field. The project was made to be lightweight and simple to setup and run.

It was developed as my graduation thesis in Computer Science between 2023/2024.

How it works

The simulator holds a state vector of qubits that can be manipulated by applying different quantum gates to it. It does not offer a wide range of resources like Qiskit, but can match the precision as long the state vector fits in ram.
